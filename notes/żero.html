David's Maltese Notes

Welcome to my notes from learning Maltese!

I personally learn best by writing out knowledge for others, and so I decided to create this series of lessons! Note that I am not a native Maltese speaker and please correct me if you find any errors. Also note that this course is massively inspired by jan Kekan San's course on Toki Pona, weird as that might sound. I think his course has a learning style that really, really works for me, and I'm excited to make use of it here :)
